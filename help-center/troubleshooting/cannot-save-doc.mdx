## Symptoms

- Save button disabled
- Error: "Unable to save changes"

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your internet connection">
    Ensure you are online. Try refreshing the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-login">
    Log out and sign back in to refresh your session.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Clearing cache solves many temporary glitches.
  </Step>
</Steps>
