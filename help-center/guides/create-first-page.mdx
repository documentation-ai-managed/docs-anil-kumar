## Steps to Create a Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    Go to your project dashboard at `https://docs.anilkumar.com`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Add Page'">
    Use the **Add Page** button to start a new page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name and write content">
    Enter a title and use Markdown to write your content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Your work saves automatically, or click **Save Now** for manual save.
  </Step>
</Steps>
