## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: GitHub account for version control integration

</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up in under 2 minutes. Follow these steps to get started.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com` and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and complete the CAPTCHA.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link from `no-reply@example.com` and click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Answer a few quick questions about your project needs and select your workspace preferences.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set Up Your Initial Workspace

Customize your workspace to match your workflow.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Browser" icon="globe">
    After login, select **New Workspace** from the sidebar.
    
    Configure basic settings:
    
````json
{
  "name": "My First Docs",
  "theme": "light",
  "features": ["mdx-support", "version-control"]
}
````
    
    Click **Create Workspace**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Desktop App" icon="monitor">
    Download the app from `https://dashboard.example.com/download`.
    
    Launch and log in. Create workspace via the welcome screen.
    
    <Callout kind="tip">
      Sync your workspace across devices automatically.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Create Your First Project

Build a documentation project with a simple MDX page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add New Project" icon="plus">
    In your workspace, click **New Project** and name it `Quickstart Docs`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create First Page" icon="file-text">
    Select **MDX Page** template. Name it `introduction.mdx`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Content" icon="edit-3">
    Paste this starter MDX:
    
    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX,Bash">
```mdx
---
title: Welcome
description: Your first page.
---

## Hello World

This is your documentation!
```

```bash
# Preview locally
npm run dev
```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="upload">
    Click **Publish** to generate a live URL like `https://docs.example.com/quickstart/introduction`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
